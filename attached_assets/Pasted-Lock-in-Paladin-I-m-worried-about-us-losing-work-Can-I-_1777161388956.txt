Game Design Reference
Core Structure
Each player brings 6 units to a match
After seeing the opponent's full roster, each player secretly picks 4 to bring into the game
Players then place their 4 units on their own 4×4 grid
Two grids face each other; units cannot cross to the opponent's grid
Action Menu (displayed top to bottom)
Move — reposition within Move Distance, costs 1 AP flat
Attack — use base attack, costs 1 AP, power 20 (scales off primary stat)
Defend — damage mitigation that turn, 0 AP change (no gain, no loss)
Wait — do nothing, gain +1 AP
Skill — use one of 4 equipped skills, variable AP cost
AP Economy
All units start at 1 AP, cap 10, carries between turns
Successful hit grants attacker +2 AP after resolution
Move costs 1 AP flat (any distance within Move Distance)
Attack costs 1 AP; Defend = 0 AP change; Wait = +1 AP
Skills have individual AP costs (1–5)
Turn Structure
Both players simultaneously select actions for all their units
Actions resolve in descending Speed order (highest Speed acts first)
Ties broken by a stable secondary key
Base Attacks
Every unit has a base attack — always available, always 20 power, 1 AP
Scales off the unit's primary attack stat (PhysAtk for melee, SpAtk for ranged)
Weaker than any skill by design
Base Attack Type is a unit stat with three options:
Melee — reaches up to 4 tiles, blocked by units in direct line
Ranged Direct — reaches up to 7 tiles, blocked by frontal defenders
Ranged Volley — reaches up to 7 tiles, bypasses frontal defenders (lobs over)
Skill Attack Styles
Melee-Direct — up to 4 tiles, blocked by intervening units
Melee-Piercing — up to 4 tiles, passes through units
Ranged-Direct — up to 8 tiles, blocked by frontal defenders
Ranged-Volleyed — up to 8 tiles, bypasses frontal defenders
Ranged-Piercing — up to 8 tiles, passes through all units in a line
Skill — custom AoE/range defined per move
Blocking Rules
A unit in a straight line (vertical or diagonal) between attacker and target blocks any Direct or standard Melee attack
Piercing and Volleyed attacks are never blocked
Units cannot deal damage to tiles adjacent to themselves — AoE always targets outward
Damage Formula
Damage = floor( ((22 × Power × AtkStat / DefStat) / 50 + 2) × rng )

rng = random float in [0.85, 1.00]
Physical moves: PhysAtk / PhysDef
Special moves: SpAtk / SpDef
Level fixed at 50 for all units
Unit Structure
Stats: HP, PhysAtk, SpAtk, PhysDef, SpDef, Speed, MoveDistance, Evasion, Base Attack Type
Skill pool: 8 moves available, player equips 4
Passive pool: 3 options, player equips 1
