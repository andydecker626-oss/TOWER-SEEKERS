# Tower Seekers — Vista Overlay Tune (lighten the murk)
## Problem
The current dark gradient overlay over the vista backgrounds is too heavy — the paintings are being muted to ~40% of their actual vibrance. Sunsets, midtones, and atmospheric detail are getting crushed.
## What to change
In `src/components/MenuShell.tsx` (or wherever the bg-overlay CSS lives), find the `.menu-shell__bg-overlay` style.
Current values:
    background: linear-gradient(
      180deg,
      rgba(7,4,15,0.55) 0%,
      rgba(7,4,15,0.70) 50%,
      rgba(7,4,15,0.85) 100%
    );
Replace with:
    background: linear-gradient(
      180deg,
      rgba(7,4,15,0.20) 0%,
      rgba(7,4,15,0.35) 50%,
      rgba(7,4,15,0.55) 100%
    );
That's the only line that changes. Everything else (bg-stack, crossfade, content animation, HUD/tab bars) stays as-is.
## Card legibility fallback
If after lightening the overlay the placeholder cards on the Towers / Town / Shop tabs feel like they're floating without enough contrast against the now-brighter bg, ALSO bump the card background opacity from `rgba(20,15,35,0.55)` to `rgba(20,15,35,0.72)`. This applies to the locked card containers — NOT the headline text, NOT the icons, NOT the borders.
If the cards already read fine after the overlay change, skip this step.
## Strict rules
- ONLY modify the overlay gradient and (optionally) the card background opacity.
- DO NOT add any blur to the bg layer or overlay.
- DO NOT change any image, route, layout, animation timing, or font.
- DO NOT touch the HUD bar or tab bar styling.
- DO NOT add a vignette, radial gradient, or any new layer.
## Acceptance criteria
After applying:
- The Towers vista (sunset valley with towers and banners) shows visible sunset color, distinct tower silhouettes, and atmospheric haze.
- The Home, Town, and Units vistas similarly come through with more visible color and depth.
- Headline text (e.g. "CHOOSE YOUR ASCENT") and placeholder card text remain legible.
- The bottom tab bar (with its own ~78% opacity bg) still cleanly separates from the painting above it.
- No layout shifts, no animation changes, no console errors.
